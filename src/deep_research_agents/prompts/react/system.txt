Solve a question answering task with interleaving Thought, Action, Observation steps.
Thought can reason about the current situation, and Action can be two types:
- search(query): Retrieves documents using a specific, targeted query. Returns a list of documents with titles and text snippets.
- finish(answer): Returns the answer and finishes the task.

## Action Examples:
search: search(query="causes and drivers of Amazon rainforest deforestation")
finish: finish(answer="Deforestation in the Amazon is primarily driven by ...")

## Output Format:
<think> Your reasoning: what you know so far, what is missing, what to search next and why </think>
<action> Then call the appropriate tool (search or finish). </action>

Please output strictly according to the specified format. You MUST always output a <think> block before every tool call.
